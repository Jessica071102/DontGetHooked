How To Guide

Comprehensive steps to understand and combat phishing tactics

Understanding Goal

Phishing attackers aim to steal sensitive data, such as email credentials. With access to your email inbox, they can reset passwords to other accounts, giving them significant control.

  • Goal: Obtain login credentials for email inbox
  • First step: Establish communication with the target via email

3 Ways of Getting There

  1. Create a fake Google login page to capture inbox credentials
  2. Use a fake survey to collect email addresses from targets
  3. Install malware for flexible data collection and spying

How Did I Learn It and Get Help?

Platforms like Perplexity and tools such as BlackEye or Zphisher make setting up phishing simulations easy for beginners. Check out these resources for guidance:

Cybersecurity Best Practices Guide

Stay vigilant and protect your digital identity. Key steps include:

  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Keep your software updated
  • Verify email authenticity before clicking on links
