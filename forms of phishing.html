Forms of Phishing

Explore the different ways cyber attackers target individuals and learn how to recognize them

Email Phishing

Email phishing involves sending fraudulent emails that appear to come from trusted sources. These emails often include malicious links or attachments that steal your personal information.

Spear Phishing

Spear phishing is a targeted form of phishing where attackers tailor their messages to specific individuals or organizations, increasing the likelihood of success.

Smishing

Smishing uses text messages to trick victims into revealing personal information or clicking on malicious links.

Vishing

Vishing, or voice phishing, involves attackers calling victims and pretending to be trusted entities to extract sensitive information.
