How to Guide

Your step-by-step guide to understand how we managed to perform our own attack.

Understanding the Goal

Obtaining sensitive information such as email addresses and passwords is the objective. With access to an email inbox password, it becomes possible to reset passwords for other linked accounts.

Goal: Gain access to the email inbox login.

The first step is to establish a connection with the target by acquiring their email address, enabling communication for subsequent interactions.


Three Ways of Getting There


  • 1. Create a fake Google login page.
  • 2. Create a fake survey to collect email addresses of my targets.
  • 3. Install malware for greater flexibility in data access and spying.

    Our fake survey

    We created a fake survey to collect email addresses by offering people an incentive for participation. This established a communication channel for sending phishing emails.

    1. Creating a Fake Survey

    We designed a survey targeting our network, such as students, by offering an incentive like a "Pizza Friday" initiative. Participants were asked to vote and provide their email addresses.

    2. Sending Phishing Emails

    Using the collected email addresses, we sent phishing emails prompting users to log in. These emails redirected recipients to a credential-harvesting website.
