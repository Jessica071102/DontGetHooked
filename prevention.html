Phishing Prevention

Learn how to protect yourself from phishing attacks and secure your online presence

Top Tips for Staying Safe

Verify Sender

Always check the sender's email address and domain for authenticity. Avoid clicking links from unknown sources.

Enable Multi-Factor Authentication

Enhance your account security by enabling multi-factor authentication wherever possible.

Use Antivirus Software

Install and update antivirus software regularly to detect and prevent malicious attacks.

Stay Informed

Keep yourself updated on the latest phishing techniques to recognize and avoid potential threats.

Use a Password Manager

Create strong, unique passwords for each account and securely store them to avoid reused passwords.

Examine URLs Carefully

Always inspect the URL before clicking. Phishing sites often use similar URLs with slight misspellings or alterations.
