What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyberattack where attackers trick individuals into revealing sensitive information like passwords or credit card details. This is often done through fake emails or websites that appear legitimate. These fraudulent messages may ask you to click on links or download files that can harm your device or steal your data. To protect yourself, always verify the sender's information and avoid clicking on suspicious links.
